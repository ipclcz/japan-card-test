How to Use · 使い方

1

Get Your Cards

Purchase Japan Trading Cards from airport vending machines across Japan. Each pack contains 1 random holographic card.

2

Open This App

Open this webpage on your phone browser (Chrome on Android, Safari on iOS). No app download needed.

3

Start AR Scan

Tap "Start AR Scan" and allow camera permission. Point your camera at the card face — hold it steady in good light.

4

Enjoy AR History

Once detected, the character's history appears in English & Japanese. Collect all 12 cards to complete the set!

Note: AR scanning uses MindAR image tracking. For best results, use a flat, well-lit card on a contrasting surface. The card image must be registered in the .mind target file — see the deploy guide below.
Deploy to GitHub Pages (Free)
1. Fork / upload this project to a GitHub repo
2. Go to Settings → Pages → Deploy from main branch
3. Your URL: https://yourname.github.io/japan-cards
4. Generate targets.mind at hiukim.github.io/mind-ar-js
5. Replace targets.mind in the repo root
